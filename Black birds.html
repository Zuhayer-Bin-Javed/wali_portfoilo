Black birds

I wake up to the black birds, The black birds singing in the dead of night. Looking out the window, Having an idea of doing something I never tried. The splashy water sounds when the speeding vehicles get over a puddle, Implied that I should get up from the cuddle and move slowly with a sound so subtle. Not intending to wake the person sleeping by, I try to hold in the tears, I try not to cry. The dissatisfaction of not being able to be with someone I truly like, Sent me to another Reich. Another realm where I am happy, where I am free, where I ride to the nearest sea shore in my red Corvette, having the one I truly want by my heavenly side. I see the bride Walking down the aisle But, she's not for me, For someone else. Probably Kyle.

Wali
